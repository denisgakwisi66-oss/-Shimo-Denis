Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about working with me

What types of projects do you typically work on?

I specialize in frontend web development (React, Vue.js, modern JavaScript) and advanced Excel solutions (dashboards, automation, data analysis). Projects range from small business websites to complex web applications and comprehensive Excel systems for data management.

What is your typical project timeline?

Timelines vary based on project complexity. Small websites: 1-2 weeks. Medium web applications: 3-6 weeks. Complex projects: 2-3 months. Excel solutions: 1-4 weeks depending on complexity. I provide detailed timelines after initial consultation.

Do you work with clients internationally?

Yes, I work with clients worldwide. All communication and collaboration happen remotely through video calls, email, and project management tools. Time zone differences are accommodated with flexible scheduling.

What is your payment process?

For fixed-price projects: 30% deposit to start, 40% at project midpoint, 30% upon completion. Hourly consulting: Invoiced weekly or bi-weekly. All payments via bank transfer, PayPal, or Wise. Detailed invoices provided for all transactions.

Do you offer ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes, I offer maintenance packages for websites and applications, and ongoing support for Excel solutions. Options include monthly retainer agreements, pay-as-you-go support, or one-time fixes. All support agreements include priority response times.
